<p><p>The Lyme disease testing market includes various diagnostic methods to detect the infection. ELISA (Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay) is commonly used for initial screening by identifying antibodies in the blood. Western Blotting is a confirmatory test that provides detailed antibody profiles. PCR (Polymerase Chain Reaction) detects the DNA of the Lyme bacteria, offering rapid and precise results. Other testing methods may include serological tests and antigen detection, each contributing uniquely to the accurate diagnosis of Lyme disease.</p></p>
