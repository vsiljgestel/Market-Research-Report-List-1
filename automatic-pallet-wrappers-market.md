<p><p>The automatic pallet wrappers market is divided into two main types: semi-automatic and full-automatic wrappers. Semi-automatic wrappers require some manual intervention, such as positioning the pallet, while the machine handles the wrapping process. In contrast, full-automatic wrappers operate independently, automatically loading and wrapping pallets without human assistance. Both types offer efficiency in packaging and safeguarding goods for transportation, with semi-automatic solutions often favored for smaller operations and full-automatic options preferred in high-volume industrial settings.</p></p>
