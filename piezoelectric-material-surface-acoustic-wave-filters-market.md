The market is primarily segmented into two types: IF SAW filters and RF SAW filters. IF SAW filters operate at intermediate frequencies, typically used in various audio and video transmission applications, while RF SAW filters function at radio frequencies, essential for wireless communication and radar systems. Both types enhance signal clarity and reduce interference, making them crucial in modern electronics.</p></p>
