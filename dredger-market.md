<p><strong>The Dredger Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cutter-Suction Dredger</li><li>Trailing Suction Hopper Dredger</li><li>Chain Bucket Dredge</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dredger market comprises various types of vessels designed for specific underwater excavation tasks. Cutter-suction dredgers use a rotating cutter head to break up seabeds and pump material through a pipeline. Trailing suction hopper dredgers are equipped with suction pipes that collect sediment while moving, storing it in a hopper for disposal. Chain bucket dredges employ a series of buckets on a conveyor chain to scoop up material from the seabed, functioning effectively in shallow waters and for heavy material.</p></p>
