<p><p>The heatsink market is primarily categorized into three types: aluminum, copper, and copper-aluminum heat sinks. Aluminum heat sinks are lightweight, cost-effective, and offer good thermal performance, making them popular for various applications. Copper heat sinks provide superior thermal conductivity, ideal for high-performance scenarios, but are heavier and more expensive. Copper-aluminum heat sinks combine the benefits of both materials, balancing weight and thermal efficiency, and are often used in specialized applications where performance and cost must be optimized.</p></p>
