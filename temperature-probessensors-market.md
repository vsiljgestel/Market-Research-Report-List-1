<p><ul><li>Non-Contact Temperature Sensors</li><li>Contact Temperature Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The temperature probes and sensors market is divided into two main types: non-contact and contact temperature sensors. Non-contact temperature sensors, such as infrared sensors, measure temperature from a distance, making them ideal for moving objects or hazardous environments. In contrast, contact temperature sensors, including thermocouples and thermistors, require direct contact with the object to provide accurate readings. Both types serve various industries, including healthcare, manufacturing, and automotive, catering to specific application needs based on accuracy and response time requirements.</p></p>
