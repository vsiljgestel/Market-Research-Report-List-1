<p><p>The construction lifters market comprises various types, primarily fixed lifters and truck-mounted lifters. Fixed lifters are stationary lifting solutions installed on-site, ideal for construction projects requiring high elevation and heavy material handling. They provide stability and efficiency for tasks like building skyscrapers or bridges. In contrast, truck-mounted lifters are mobile units that can be easily transported, offering versatility and quick setup for various job sites. They are suitable for tasks demanding flexibility and rapid deployment in diverse environments.</p></p>
