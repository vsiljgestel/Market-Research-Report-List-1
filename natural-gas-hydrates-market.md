<p><p>Natural gas hydrates are crystalline compounds formed from water and natural gas, predominantly found in two primary markets: onshore and offshore/marine. Onshore gas hydrates are located in permafrost regions and sedimentary basins, making exploration and extraction potentially easier and more cost-effective. In contrast, offshore/marine gas hydrates are found in seabed sediments, presenting unique challenges due to water depths and harsher conditions. Both markets hold significant energy potential, with ongoing research and development aimed at unlocking their resources.</p></p>
