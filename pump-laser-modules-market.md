<p><ul><li>Single Mode Pump Lasers</li><li>Multi-Mode Pump Lasers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pump laser modules market consists of two primary types: single-mode and multi-mode pump lasers. Single-mode pump lasers utilize a single optical fiber core, providing high precision and efficiency, making them ideal for long-distance communication and high-performance applications. In contrast, multi-mode pump lasers feature multiple cores, allowing them to transmit larger volumes of data over shorter distances, which is beneficial in applications requiring high power but at reduced complexity and cost. Each type serves distinct needs within various industries.</p></p>
