<p><p>The gastric pump market comprises two main types: disposable and reusable pumps. Disposable gastric pumps are designed for single use, ensuring enhanced hygiene and minimizing the risk of cross-contamination. They are often favored in acute care settings where quick turnover is crucial. In contrast, reusable gastric pumps are durable and can be cleaned and sterilized for multiple uses, making them cost-effective for long-term applications. Both types cater to different needs in healthcare, emphasizing patient safety and operational efficiency.</p></p>
