<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Retinal Drugs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet AMD</li><li>Diabetic Retinopathy</li><li>DME</li><li>RVO</li><li>Mcnv</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The retinal drugs market encompasses various conditions affecting the retina. Wet age-related macular degeneration (AMD) involves abnormal blood vessel growth leading to vision loss. Diabetic retinopathy results from diabetes-induced retinal damage affecting vision. Diabetic macular edema (DME) refers to fluid accumulation in the retina due to diabetes. Retinal vein occlusion (RVO) occurs when retinal veins become blocked, causing vision issues. Lastly, macular choroidal neovascularization (Mcnv) involves the formation of new blood vessels in the macula, impacting vision quality.</p></p>
