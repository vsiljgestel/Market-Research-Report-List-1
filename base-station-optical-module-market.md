<p><p>The Base Station Optical Module market encompasses three main types: Optical Receiver Modules, Optical Transmitter Modules, and Optical Transceiver Modules. Optical Receiver Modules are designed to convert incoming optical signals into electrical signals, ensuring data is accurately received. Conversely, Optical Transmitter Modules convert electrical signals into optical signals for transmission. Optical Transceiver Modules combine both functionalities, allowing for bidirectional communication in a single unit. These modules are essential for enhancing data transfer rates and network performance in telecommunications infrastructure.</p></p>
