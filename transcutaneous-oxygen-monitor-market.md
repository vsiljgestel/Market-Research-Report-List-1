<p><p>A Transcutaneous Oxygen Monitor is a non-invasive device that measures the oxygen levels in the blood through the skin. This technology is particularly useful for continuous monitoring in critical care settings, neonatal units, and during surgery. It works by applying a small electrode to the skin, which heats the area to increase blood flow, allowing for accurate readings of oxygen saturation levels in the underlying tissues. </p><p>The Transcutaneous Oxygen Monitor Market is experiencing significant growth driven by the increasing prevalence of respiratory diseases, advancements in healthcare technologies, and the rising need for continuous patient monitoring.
