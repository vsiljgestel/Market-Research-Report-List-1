<p><ul><li>Non-integrated Smart Sensor</li><li>Hybrid Smart Sensor</li><li>Integrated Smart Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smart sensor chips market comprises three main types: non-integrated, hybrid, and integrated smart sensors. Non-integrated smart sensors operate independently, requiring separate components for data processing. Hybrid smart sensors combine multiple sensing technologies in a single package, enhancing functionality while still keeping some processing external. Integrated smart sensors incorporate both sensing and processing capabilities in one unit, leading to better performance, reduced size, and lower power consumption, making them ideal for modern applications in IoT and automation.</p></p>
