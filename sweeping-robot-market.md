In contrast, ultrasonic bionic technology employs sound waves to map the environment and identify distances, enabling precise movements and better spatial awareness. Both technologies enhance the performance and autonomy of sweeping robots, catering to consumer preferences for efficient and intelligent cleaning solutions.</p></p>
