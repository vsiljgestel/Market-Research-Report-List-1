<p><ul><li>Steel Laminated Cores</li><li>Solid Cores</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Core transformers are essential components in electrical systems, designed to efficiently manage magnetic fields. The market features two primary types: steel laminated cores and solid cores. Steel laminated cores are constructed from thin sheets of electrical steel, reducing energy losses through magnetic hysteresis and eddy currents, making them suitable for applications requiring high efficiency. In contrast, solid cores, made from a single piece of material, provide robust performance but may have higher energy losses. Both types serve distinct demands in electrical engineering.</p></p>
