<p><ul><li>Granular</li><li>Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Poly Tetra Fluoroethylene (PTFE) market is primarily segmented into two types: granular and powder. Granular PTFE is often used in applications requiring high lubricity and low friction, such as bearings and seals, because of its bulkier form. In contrast, powder PTFE is utilized for coatings and applications where fine particle size enhances dispersibility and adhesion, such as in non-stick cookware and industrial components. Both forms cater to diverse industrial needs, requiring specific properties for optimal performance.</p></p>
