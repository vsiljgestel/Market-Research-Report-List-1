<p><ul><li>Magnetic Peripheral Pump</li><li>Centrifugal Peripheral Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The peripheral pumps market consists of two main types: magnetic peripheral pumps and centrifugal peripheral pumps. Magnetic peripheral pumps utilize a magnetic coupling to eliminate pump leakage and enhance efficiency, making them ideal for handling sensitive fluids. In contrast, centrifugal peripheral pumps use the centrifugal force generated by a rotating impeller to move fluids, providing consistent flow rates and high performance in various applications. Both types cater to specific needs across industries, emphasizing efficiency and reliability in fluid handling.</p></p>
