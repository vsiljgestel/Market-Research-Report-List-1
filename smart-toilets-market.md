<p><p>The smart toilets market is primarily categorized into two types: smart toilet seats and integrated smart toilets. Smart toilet seats are retrofit devices designed to enhance traditional toilets, providing features like heated seating, bidet functions, and customizable controls. In contrast, integrated smart toilets combine the bowl and seat into a single unit, offering advanced functionalities such as automatic flushing, built-in bidets, and self-cleaning mechanisms. Both types aim to improve hygiene, comfort, and sustainability in modern bathrooms.</p></p>
