<p><ul><li>External Beam Radiation Therapy (EBRT)</li><li>Brachytherapy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The radiation oncology market primarily comprises two types: External Beam Radiation Therapy (EBRT) and Brachytherapy. EBRT involves delivering high-energy beams from outside the body to targeted tumor sites, providing effective treatment with precision. This method is widely utilized for various cancers and includes advanced techniques like IMRT and SRS. Conversely, Brachytherapy involves placing radioactive sources directly inside or near the tumor, allowing localized treatment with minimal damage to surrounding healthy tissue. Both methods are essential for effective cancer management.</p></p>
