<p><p>Amino acids-based biostimulants are categorized into three primary market types: foliar treatment, soil treatment, and seed treatment. Foliar treatment involves the application of amino acids directly onto plant leaves, enhancing nutrient absorption and stress response. Soil treatment focuses on enriching the soil with amino acids to improve microbial activity and nutrient availability. Seed treatment coats seeds with amino acids to promote germination and early plant development. Each method offers unique benefits for plant growth and overall agricultural productivity.</p></p>
