<p><strong>The Acoustic Wave Delay Line Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Acoustic Wave</li><li>Bulk Acoustic Wave</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The acoustic wave delay line market is primarily divided into two types: Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) and Bulk Acoustic Wave (BAW) devices. SAW devices utilize surface waves traveling along a material's surface and are commonly used in filters and sensors due to their compact size and low cost. In contrast, BAW devices operate through waves propagating within the bulk of a material, offering higher frequency performance and better efficiency, making them suitable for advanced applications in telecommunications and radar systems.</p></p>
