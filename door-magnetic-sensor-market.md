<p><p>A door magnetic sensor is a security device used to detect the opening and closing of doors and windows. It typically consists of two components: a magnet and a sensor. When the door is closed, the magnet and sensor are aligned, keeping the circuit closed. When the door opens, the alignment is disrupted, triggering an alarm or alert in a security system. These sensors are widely used in residential, commercial, and industrial applications to enhance security and automate systems.</p><p>The Door Magnetic Sensor Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.9% during the forecast period.
