<p><strong>The Pharmaceutical Cleaning Validation Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Sampling</li><li>Indirect Sampling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pharmaceutical cleaning validation market encompasses two primary sampling methods: direct and indirect sampling. Direct sampling involves collecting samples from surfaces or equipment immediately after cleaning to assess residue levels, ensuring compliance with regulatory standards. In contrast, indirect sampling evaluates cleaning effectiveness indirectly by analyzing rinse water or swab samples to detect contaminants. Both approaches are critical for maintaining product integrity, ensuring safety, and preventing cross-contamination, thereby supporting the overall quality assurance processes in the pharmaceutical industry.</p></p>
