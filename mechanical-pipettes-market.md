<p><strong>The Mechanical Pipettes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Channel Pipette</li><li>Multi-Channel Pipette</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mechanical pipettes market is divided into two main types: single-channel and multi-channel pipettes. Single-channel pipettes offer precise liquid handling for individual samples, making them ideal for routine assays and applications requiring accuracy. In contrast, multi-channel pipettes, equipped with multiple tips, allow simultaneous dispensing across several samples, enhancing efficiency in high-throughput tasks like 96-well plate experiments. Both types are essential in laboratories, supporting various applications in research, clinical, and quality control environments.</p></p>
