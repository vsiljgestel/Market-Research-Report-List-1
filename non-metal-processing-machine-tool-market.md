<p><p>The Non-metal Processing Machine Tool market encompasses two primary types: Cutting and Forming. Cutting tools are designed to remove material from non-metallic workpieces, utilizing various techniques like milling and slicing to achieve precise shapes and finishes. On the other hand, Forming tools reshape materials without material removal, employing methods such as bending, stretching, or compressing. Both types cater to diverse industries, facilitating the production of intricate components while enhancing efficiency and accuracy in non-metal machining processes.</p></p>
