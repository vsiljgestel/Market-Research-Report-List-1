<p><p>Anaesthetic gas scavenging systems are essential for capturing excess anaesthetic gases during surgical procedures to ensure safety and environmental compliance. The market features two main types: single pump and double pump systems. Single pump systems utilize one motor to create suction, making them simpler and often more cost-effective. In contrast, double pump systems use two motors for enhanced efficiency and greater gas extraction capability, ideal for larger or busier operating rooms, providing better control of gas levels and improved safety.</p></p>
