<p><strong>The Oil and Gas Pipeline Leak Detection System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Acoustic/Ultrasonic</li><li>E-RTTM</li><li>Fiber-Optic</li><li>Vapor Sensing</li><li>Mass/Volume Balance</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oil and gas pipeline leak detection system market consists of various technologies. Acoustic/ultrasonic systems utilize sound waves to detect leaks by measuring fluctuations in pipeline noise. E-RTTM (Enhanced Real-Time Transient Modeling) analyzes flow data for anomalies. Fiber-optic systems use light transmission to sense temperature changes indicative of leaks. Vapor sensing detects gas emissions through vapor analysis. Mass/volume balance systems monitor discrepancies between input and output, identifying potential leaks through balance inconsistencies, ensuring pipeline safety and integrity.</p></p>
