<p><p>The carbon steel ball valves market is categorized into three main types: One Piece, Two Piece, and Three Piece valves. One Piece carbon steel ball valves are designed as a single unit, offering high strength and compactness, ideal for low-pressure applications. Two Piece valves consist of two body components, providing easier maintenance and repair. Three Piece valves feature a design that allows for quick disassembly for maintenance and are versatile, suitable for various pressures and services in industrial applications.</p></p>
