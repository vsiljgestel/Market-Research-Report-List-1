<p><p>The artificial graphite powder market is primarily divided into two types: primary synthetic graphite and secondary synthetic graphite. Primary synthetic graphite is produced from high-purity petroleum coke through a high-temperature process, offering superior thermal and electrical conductivity, making it ideal for applications like batteries and electrodes. Secondary synthetic graphite, on the other hand, is derived from recycling carbon-containing materials, enhancing sustainability by reusing resources. Both types play crucial roles in industries such as energy storage, electronics, and manufacturing.</p></p>
