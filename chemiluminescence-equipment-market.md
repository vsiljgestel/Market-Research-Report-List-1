<p><strong>The Chemiluminescence Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Luminescence</li><li>Enzymatic Luminescence</li><li>Electrochemiluminescence</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The chemiluminescence equipment market is segmented into three main types: Direct Luminescence, Enzymatic Luminescence, and Electrochemiluminescence. Direct Luminescence involves the emission of light without any intermediate reactions, often used in simple assays. Enzymatic Luminescence utilizes enzyme-catalyzed reactions to produce light, making it suitable for biochemical applications. Electrochemiluminescence combines electrochemical reactions with luminescent output, allowing for highly sensitive detections, primarily in clinical diagnostics and environmental monitoring. Each type has distinct applications based on sensitivity, specificity, and detection requirements.</p></p>
