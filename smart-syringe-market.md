<p><ul><li>Auto-disable syringes</li><li>Active safety syringes</li><li>Passive safety syringes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smart syringe market is categorized into three main types: auto-disable syringes, active safety syringes, and passive safety syringes. Auto-disable syringes automatically lock after a single use, preventing reuse and reducing infection risk. Active safety syringes feature mechanisms that require user intervention to activate safety features, offering flexibility during use. Passive safety syringes incorporate automatic protection systems that engage without user action, enhancing safety by minimizing exposure to needles. Together, these innovations aim to improve patient safety and compliance in healthcare settings.</p></p>
