<p><p>Deadweight testers are devices used for pressure calibration and measurement, utilizing the weight of masses to exert pressure on a fluid. The market for these testers is segmented into pneumatic and hydraulic types. Pneumatic deadweight testers use compressed gas to create pressure, suitable for lower pressure ranges, while hydraulic deadweight testers operate with liquids, allowing for higher pressure applications. Both markets serve industries like aerospace, oil and gas, and manufacturing, ensuring precise pressure calibration for various equipment and systems.</p></p>
