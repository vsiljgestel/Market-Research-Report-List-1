<p><p>The Food and Beverage Stevia market comprises three main types: liquid extract, powder extract, and leaf extract. Liquid extract is a concentrated form often used in beverages and ready-to-eat products for its ease of use and quick dissolution. Powder extract is versatile, suitable for both baking and beverages, offering a longer shelf life. Leaf extract involves using whole or dried stevia leaves, providing a more natural option but has a stronger flavor profile and lower solubility than the other forms.</p></p>
