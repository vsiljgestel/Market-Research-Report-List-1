<p><p>The steel bending machines market comprises three main types: manual, hydraulic, and CNC bending machines. Manual bending machines require skilled operators to bend steel using hand-operated mechanisms, offering simplicity and low cost. Hydraulic steel bending machines leverage hydraulic power for enhanced force and precision, suitable for heavy-duty applications. CNC steel bending machines utilize computer-controlled technology for advanced automation, allowing intricate designs and high-volume production with accuracy. Each type serves distinct needs in the construction and manufacturing sectors.</p></p>
