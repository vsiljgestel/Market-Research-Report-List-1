<p><p>The skin toner market consists of various types, including skin bracers or fresheners, skin tonics, and astringents. Skin bracers are gentle, hydrating products that refresh the skin without stripping its natural moisture, often containing soothing ingredients. Skin tonics offer more balanced cleansing, targeting specific concerns, while providing nourishment and hydration. Astringents, on the other hand, have a higher alcohol content and work to tighten pores and remove excess oil, making them ideal for oily or acne-prone skin.</p></p>
