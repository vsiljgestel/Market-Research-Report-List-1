<p><p>The water treatment equipment and systems market includes various technologies that ensure safe and clean water. Pumps facilitate the movement of water through treatment processes. UV systems utilize ultraviolet light to disinfect water by targeting microorganisms. Ozone systems introduce ozone gas for oxidation and purification. Underdrain filtration systems collect filtered water post-filtration. Aeration systems enhance oxygen absorption to eliminate contaminants. Chlorine dosing systems add chlorine for disinfection, while clarification systems separate solids from water for clearer output, vital for water quality management.</p></p>
