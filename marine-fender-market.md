<p><p>The marine fender market comprises various types designed for specific applications. Rubber fenders are widely used for their durability and shock absorption. Foam fenders offer lightweight, non-marking protection, ideal for smaller vessels. HALO fenders provide enhanced performance with integrated protection features. Pneumatic fenders are air-filled, offering exceptional energy absorption for larger ships. Tug fenders are specifically designed for towing operations, ensuring effective protection during maneuvering. Each type serves unique operational needs, enhancing marine safety and efficiency.</p></p>
