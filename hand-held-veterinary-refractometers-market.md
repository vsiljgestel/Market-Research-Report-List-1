<p><ul><li>Optical Refractometers</li><li>Digital Refractometers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hand-held veterinary refractometer market is divided into two main types: optical refractometers and digital refractometers. Optical refractometers use lenses and light to measure the refraction of light passing through a liquid sample, providing readings based on scale markings. In contrast, digital refractometers utilize electronic sensors to provide precise digital readings, often with added features like temperature compensation and easier data storage. Both types enable veterinarians to assess hydration levels and diagnose conditions in animals accurately.</p></p>
