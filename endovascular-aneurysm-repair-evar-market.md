<p><p>Endovascular Aneurysm Repair (EVAR) is a minimally invasive surgical technique used to treat abdominal aortic aneurysms (AAAs). It involves the insertion of a stent-graft via the femoral artery, which is positioned within the aorta to reinforce the vessel wall and prevent rupture. The benefits of EVAR over traditional open surgery include reduced recovery times, less postoperative pain, and lower risk of complications.</p><p>The EVAR market is experiencing significant growth, driven by an aging population and increasing prevalence of cardiovascular diseases. Advancements in technology, including improved imaging techniques and the development of more sophisticated stent-grafts, are further propelling market expansion.
