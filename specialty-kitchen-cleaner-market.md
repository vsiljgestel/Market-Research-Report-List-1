<p><strong>The Specialty Kitchen Cleaner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Neutral Cleaner</li><li>Alkaline Cleaner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The specialty kitchen cleaner market is segmented primarily into two types: neutral cleaners and alkaline cleaners. Neutral cleaners are pH-balanced products suitable for various surfaces, making them safe for sensitive materials like granite and stainless steel. They effectively remove grease and grime without damaging surfaces. Alkaline cleaners, on the other hand, have a higher pH and are designed for tougher cleaning tasks, breaking down fats and proteins, making them ideal for ovens and heavy-duty kitchen equipment. Both types cater to diverse cleaning needs in kitchens.</p></p>
