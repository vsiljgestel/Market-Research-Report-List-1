<p><p>The Automotive Gasoline Direct Injection (GDI) device market consists of two main types: mechanical and electronic systems. In contrast, electronic GDI systems leverage advanced electronic control units (ECUs) for precise fuel injection timing and quantity, enhancing engine performance and efficiency.
